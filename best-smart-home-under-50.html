Tips for Buying Budget Smart Home Devices

  • Wait for sales. Amazon devices drop 40‚Äì60% on Prime Day and Black Friday. Wyze products go on sale monthly. Check our deals page before buying anything.
  • Avoid no-name brands. "Smart plug" products from brands you've never heard of often have terrible apps, no security updates, and die within a year. Stick with Wyze, TP-Link/Kasa, SwitchBot, IKEA, Govee, and Amazon/Google/Apple for budget picks.
  • Check for subscriptions. Some budget cameras and doorbells are cheap upfront but require monthly subscriptions for basic features like cloud storage. Every product on this list works without a subscription (or offers meaningful free tiers).
  • Start with what annoys you. Identify the one thing in your home that frustrates you daily ‚Äî forgetting lights, no visibility at the front door, a freezing house when you wake up. Solve that first. The enthusiasm from fixing one thing will drive you to build out the rest.
