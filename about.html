How We Test

Every product recommendation on this site has been physically tested. We don't rely on press releases or manufacturer claims. Here's our process:

  • We buy retail. No manufacturer-provided review units. We buy everything at full price from the same stores you use, so our experience matches yours.
  • We test for weeks, not hours. A smart thermostat that works great on day one might have connectivity issues on day 15. We run extended tests to catch real-world problems.
  • We test compatibility. A smart plug in isolation is easy. A smart plug that needs to work with your Echo, your Google Home, and your SmartThings hub? That's where things get interesting. We test cross-ecosystem compatibility because that's how people actually use these devices.
  • We consider the total cost. A $99 camera with a $100/year subscription isn't a $99 camera. We factor in subscription fees, hub requirements, and ecosystem lock-in when making recommendations.
